Tom Hackshaw

Tom Hackshaw is a web designer and pre-service highschool teacher in Tāmaki Makaurau, Aotearoa. A believer in the Open Web, he enjoys creating resources and tools that can be shared, changed and built upon by others. If you would like, take a look at his resumé.

He has previously worked for: Michael Parekowhai, Sanderson Contemporary, ART+OBJECT, Elam School of Fine Arts, and volunteered/trained with Artspace Aotearoa, Mozilla and Lynfield College.
